TO DO LIST

A to-do list is a list of tasks that need to be completed, usually organized by priority.

Microproductivity

This approach allows individuals to efficiently tick off items from their to-do lists, making the overall workload feel less daunting and reducing procrastination. It focuses on accomplishing small but significant tasks quickly, which can lead to a sense of achievement and increased motivation.

Plan your time

To create a productive to-do list, it can be helpful to know your schedule, choose a suitable format, consider daily activities as tasks, list tasks starting with an action verb, order them by priority and regularly check off completed tasks.
